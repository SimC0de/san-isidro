About the Project

This capstone project involves the development of a school portal aimed at improving internal communication and information management within the school community. The portal is a web-based platform that consolidates various functions such as posting announcements, viewing class schedules, accessing learning materials, and distributing other school-related updates.

The system is designed to be accessible to students, teachers, and parents, allowing each user group to retrieve relevant information through a single interface. Its core purpose is to streamline the flow of information and provide a more organized method for sharing academic and administrative content.

By offering centralized access to essential resources, the portal addresses common communication challenges faced in schools and supports a more connected educational environment. The project seeks to contribute to the efficiency of school operations and promote timely information dissemination across different members of the school community.
